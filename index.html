I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Virginia, advised by Wes Weimer. I research software acceptability and human factors in software engineering. I am interested in understanding how humans perceive and interact with programs, especially how tools can support or interfere with program comprehension. My interests also include program analysis and transformations to improve acceptability.

My dissertation proposal, titled "Improving Program Acceptability Through Source Code Transformations," was approved in April 2016. [PDF] [slides]
